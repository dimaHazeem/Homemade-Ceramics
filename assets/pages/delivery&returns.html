Return Guidelines

Return Window

Returns are accepted within 14 days if the item is unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging.

Damaged Items

If your ceramic item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with clear photos of the item and packaging.

Small variations in glaze, size, or texture are not defects. They are part of the handmade ceramic process.
